About BookmarkD

Letterboxd for books - Track, rate, and share your reading journey.

What is BookmarkD?

BookmarkD is a social book tracking platform that helps readers discover, rate, and discuss books. Share your reading journey with friends, get personalized recommendations, and join a community of book lovers.

Features

  • Rate books on a 1-10 scale
  • Write and share reviews
  • Follow other readers
  • Create custom reading lists
  • Track reading status (to-read, reading, finished)
  • Get personalized recommendations
  • Import from Goodreads
  • Year-in-review statistics

Tech Stack

Built with Go, PostgreSQL, and vanilla JavaScript. Open source and privacy-focused.
